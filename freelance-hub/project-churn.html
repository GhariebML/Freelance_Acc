Customer Churn Prediction

Predictive analytics using XGBoost to identify at-risk SaaS subscriptions.

The Challenge

High customer acquisition costs made retention a critical business priority. The marketing team needed a way to proactively identify unhappy customers before they canceled their subscription.

The Solution

I conducted advanced Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on historical user behavior, performed feature engineering, and built a high-accuracy XGBoost classifier to predict churn probability.

The Outcome

The final model achieved an 85% recall rate. Model explainability (SHAP values) was used to tell stakeholders *why* customers were churning, allowing for targeted retention campaigns.

Technologies Used

Python | XGBoost | Jupyter Notebook | Seaborn & Matplotlib
